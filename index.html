Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is airfreight delivery?

Most airfreight shipments arrive within 24â€“48 hours, depending on the destination, flight availability, and customs processing. We prioritize speed and reliability, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination as quickly as possible. Expedited options are available for urgent shipments.

Do you handle international customs?

Yes, we provide comprehensive customs clearance services for all international shipments. Our experienced team navigates complex regulations and documentation to ensure smooth border transitions, minimizing delays and avoiding compliance issues.

Can I track my cargo in real time?

Absolutely. Our advanced tracking system offers live updates, GPS location data, and status notifications throughout the journey. You'll have full visibility from pickup to final delivery, accessible via our online portal or mobile app.

Do you offer temperature-controlled shipping?

Yes, we specialize in cold-chain logistics tailored for sensitive goods such as pharmaceuticals, vaccines, fresh produce, and other perishables. Our temperature-controlled containers and monitoring systems maintain optimal conditions throughout transit.

Is weekend delivery available?

Weekend delivery is available in select regions and for eligible service tiers. To confirm availability for your shipment, please contact our support team. We strive to accommodate your schedule with flexible delivery options.
